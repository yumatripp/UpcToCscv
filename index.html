Record Barcode to CSV

Scan a UPC/EAN barcode on a record sleeve, look up the release, preview the tracks, edit BPM and key inline, batch records into one session, and export a clean CSV. MusicBrainz is used first, with Discogs as a backup when you provide a Discogs token.
Camera scan works best in Chrome or Edge on supported devices. If scanning is unavailable, you can still type or paste the barcode manually. Discogs backup lookup requires a Discogs user token.
